Who We Are

The Black Student Union (BSU) at Estrella Mountain Community College is a collective created to provide a safe space for African American students' progression at EMCC. We discuss social, political, and economic standpoints in our community… and we have FUN!

Bi-Weekly General Meetings: Bi-Weekly on Thursdays 2:30-4pm in Estrella Hall S136 (First Floor).
